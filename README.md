

Siddhnath Car Care Billing

A fully offline Progressive Web App (PWA) for managing car service bills.

## Features
- Create & manage service invoices
- Generate professional PDF invoices
- Share invoices directly on WhatsApp
- Auto-upload PDFs to Google Drive
- Works offline — all data saved on device
- Installable on Android home screen
- Marathi language support

## Usage
Open the link in Chrome on your phone and tap "Add to Home Screen" to install.

Built for Siddhnath Car Care, Mhavashi, Patan, Maharashtra.
